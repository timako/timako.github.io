About

Academic Profile

I am at Zhejiang University, working around computer graphics and vision. My current interests include real-time rendering, physically based rendering, 3D reconstruction, neural visual representation, and practical graphics systems.

This site replaces my old notes-oriented homepage and now serves as a concise research profile for academic communication.

Interests

Rendering Systems

Computer vision and generative modeling methods for visual content understanding, synthesis, and editing.
